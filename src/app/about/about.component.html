About Around Whitefield

Our Mission

Around Whitefield aims to be the definitive guide for residents and visitors to discover the best of what Whitefield has to offer - from dining and shopping to recreation and entertainment.

We're passionate about highlighting local businesses, community events, and hidden gems that make Whitefield special. Our goal is to connect people with great experiences in their neighborhood.

The Whitefield Story

Once a quiet settlement established by the Eurasian Christian Community, Whitefield has transformed into one of Bangalore's most vibrant and rapidly developing suburbs, home to major tech parks, residential communities, and commercial establishments.

Named after D.S. White, the founder of the European and Anglo-Indian Settlement, Whitefield has retained some of its historical charm while evolving into a modern tech hub.
